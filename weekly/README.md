# Weekly Engineering AI Synthesis

Create weekly synthesis notes as `weekly/YYYY-Www.md`.

Weekly notes should convert daily radar items into a research judgment, not merely repeat links.

```markdown
# Weekly Engineering AI Synthesis — YYYY-Www

## Main Thesis
One sentence describing the week's most important technical movement.

## Signals
1. Signal with evidence and links.
2. Signal with evidence and links.
3. Signal with evidence and links.

## What Changed?
What is different compared with previous weeks: new benchmark, new failure mode, new representation, new workflow pattern, or new deployment evidence.

## VA Research Opportunities
- Experiment idea.
- Literature-review angle.
- Benchmark or validation metric to build.
- Repo/tooling improvement.

## LinkedIn / Blog Candidate
A short public-facing thesis and outline. Keep hype low and engineering judgment high.

## Updated Repository Areas
- `daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- `resources/...`
- `themes/...`
- `experiments/...`
```

A good weekly synthesis should answer: **what should VA think, read, test, or write next?**
