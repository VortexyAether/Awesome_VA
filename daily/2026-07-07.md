- Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29702
- Type: Paper / SciML derivative infrastructure for PINNs and discretized PDE adjoints
- Why it matters: General-purpose AD is convenient, but high-order PDE residuals and CFD adjoint operators can pay large runtime/memory costs.
